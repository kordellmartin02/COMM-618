This article may be a great resource for those looking to purchase a home. However, there is a lot of economic and real estate jargon that may be too difficult or wordy for readers to understand. When we look at the graphs, I would have preferred the first to be separated. Though it does look cool, trying to move your mouse through the lines of each topic is difficult. Other graphs included in the piece are very helpful to figure out how much homes cost by county in the United States or abroad. This story has so much data that it’s almost too much to be compacted into one article that is consistently updated. Listing prices and buying power are great to piece together for comparison, but it would have been interesting if they explained why purchasing a home has become such a financial challenge in the 2020s more than ever before, because of the lack of purchasing power after the pandemic. I do think this would have added to the already large amount of content in the article, but it would have been nice to mention that. NBC is also very open about where they're getting this data from; they attribute the sources in the article, even introducing one in the fourth paragraph of the article.
