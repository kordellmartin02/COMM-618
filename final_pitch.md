Do test (D.C. CAPE) scores and graduation rates align with funding in D.C. schools? I also want to find the relationship between public funding and private funding and the scores students get in public schools, public charter schools, and private schools. Are students who attend certain schools with more funding guaranteed higher test scores in math and literacy? Is there any relation between the quality of education students receive and funding?
