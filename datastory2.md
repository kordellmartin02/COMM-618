I like the article being framed by waterways that represent the body of water. The information is also spaced out, which is great for readers like me who don’t know quite a lot about the conflict and its consequences on oil and gas prices. I’m a little thrown off by the formatting of the piece since the background of the Strait of Hormuz comes after some of the impacts of the war have already been visited. So when you reach the background section, you may have to scroll back up to fully grasp the effects stated earlier in the article. Reading through the piece is quick, and you can really understand the effects of the war since they're laid out separately in individual paragraphs.
